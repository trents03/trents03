Volleyball

Greatest Sport Ever?

Since the age of 4 Volleyball has been one of the pillars of my life. It has brought me some of my closest friends and taught me some of the most important lessons in life.

Competition Teamwork Energy Discipline

What position is right for you?

  1. Setter
    • You act as the "quarterback" and get to touch the ball on almost every play.
    • It's rewarding to trick the opposing blockers with a fast set.
  2. Libero/Defensive Specialist
    • You get to make "spectacular" diving saves and keep the play alive.
    • There is a huge rush in digging a hard-driven spike from the opponent.
  3. Outside Hitter
    • You are often the go-to attacker for high-pressure situations.
    • Nothing beats the feeling of a perfect line shot or a cross-court kill.
  4. Middle Blocker
    • There is nothing like the "roof" block—the feeling of perfectly timing a jump and sending the opponent's hard-driven spike straight back down at their feet.
    • You get to run "quick" sets that happen so fast the other team doesn't even have time to jump, leaving the floor open for an easy kill.
  5. Opposite Hitter
    • You get the thrill of being the "shut-down" defender against the opponent’s best outside hitter, making them second-guess every swing.
    • As a versatile attacker, you get the "big-air" rush of flying in from the back row for a powerful "D-ball" attack that catches the defense off guard.
  6. Serving Specialist
    • You enter the game in the highest-pressure moments specifically to go on a "run," feeling the momentum shift as you rip an ace or force an out-of-system pass.
    • There is a unique satisfaction in watching the opposing passers scramble and look at each other in confusion after a perfectly placed short serve or a heavy top-spin jump serve.
